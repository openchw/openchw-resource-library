OpenCHW Resource Library

Approved public materials for caregiver support, senior center programs, pilot resources, grants, forms, and reference documents.

Purpose

This site is a public reference library for final, approved OpenCHW materials. Drafts and working documents should stay in Google Workspace or internal project folders until approved for public use.

Use stable PDF URLs from this site in OpenCHW CSV files, resource directories, program content, and referral-support materials.

Publication rule

Only upload materials that are approved for public viewing. Do not upload private caregiver data, case notes, protected health information, internal credentials, private partner lists, or draft documents that have not been approved by the program owner.
